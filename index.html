About me

My name is Anyadiegwu Charley Iyke. I am a lecturer and I hold Bachelors, Masters, and Doctorate Degrees in Petroleum and Gas Engineering.

I have written a book published by international publishers. I have also written and co-authored over 50 research and review publications on oil and gas production and storage in leading international peer-reviewed journals. I have acquired over twenty-five years of working experience that covers Academic, Oil and Gas Production, Processing and Administration.

I am a member of Council for Registered Engineers of Nigeria (COREN) and Society of Petroleum Engineers, International (SPE).
